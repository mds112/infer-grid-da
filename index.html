Website Overview

Infer Grid is a Data Analytics tool created with data tracking , inference and compilation in mind . The earliest intentions for this website were to be used an analytics tool in motorsports such as F1, Rally and such where a minute difference may prove to be Consequential and Imperative . Race Engineers can upload the vehicle data directly onto this platform to analyse it , compile it and even ask the platform to provide with an inference and suggest areas for improvement
