Website Overview

Infer Grid is a Data Analytics tool created with data tracking, inference and compilation in mind. The earliest intentions for this website were to be used as an analytics tool in motorsports such as F1, Rally and such where a minute difference may prove to be consequential and imperative. Race Engineers can upload the vehicle data directly onto this platform to analyse it, compile it and even ask the platform to provide an inference and suggest areas for improvement.
